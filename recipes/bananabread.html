Banana Bread

Protein Dessert

A healthier version of the classic dessert made with only one banana

very easy to make. Preparation Cook time - 50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 75g Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 30g Sugar Free Syrup
  • Splash of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup or 60 g of Flour
  • Protein powder
  • 1 Tsp of Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup of Erythritol
  • 15g of Chocolate

Instructions

  1. Mash the banana in a bowl
  2. Add the yogurt, syrup, vanilla extract and eggs and mix well
  3. Add the flour, protein powder, baking powder, cinnamon and erythritol and mix well
  4. Chop the chocolate into small pieces and add to the mixture
  5. Pour the mixture into a loaf tin lined with parchment paper
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  7. Bake for 50 minutes
  8. Let cool for 30 minutes
